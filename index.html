Cohesion Summit #2

Thursday, October 9th, 2025 · 6-10 PM
Cecil Community Centre · Toronto
Add to Calendar

Come with an idea, dream, or project, leave with energy and collaborators!

Welcome to the second installment of the Cohesion Summit series. When people work separately to tackle the same problem, unaware of each other, they often do the same things with less resources and miss out on collaboration. Our mission is to bring people together, in-person, to work together, breaking down the fake silos.

You will be in a room with a range of people across sectors and life experiences, and through creativity, will-power, and FUN, find something to unite each other and form a cohesive team!

What this event isn't:

  • A hackathon
  • A corporate networking event

What this event will have you do:

  • Find the intersection between your self/project and other people around you
  • Connect with people who have interesting new ideas
  • HAVE FUN!
