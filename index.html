What is Cohesion Summit?

An energizing evening where Toronto's community builders, innovators, and dreamers unite to transform solo visions into collaborative realities. This isn't just another networking eventâ€”it's a structured experience designed to help you find your perfect collaborators and launch projects that bring people together.

The Experience:
6:00 PM - Speed Pitching Icebreakers
You'll rotate through small groups, practicing your pitch and hearing others' dreams. Each person gets one minute to share their project, idea, or vision. It's fast-paced, fun, and helps everyone refine their message while discovering potential synergies.

6:45 PM - Team Formation
Armed with clarity from the icebreakers, you'll write your idea on a sign and find 3-9 others who share your passion. Teams form organically around the most compelling visions and complementary skills.

7:15 PM - Public Pitches & Connection Time
Each team presents their vision to the full summit. After pitches, the room transforms into a connection hub where you can join forces with the teams that inspired you most.

Come with your idea. Leave with your team. Whether you're building a neighborhood app, organizing community gatherings, or dreaming up the next big platform for human connectionâ€”this is where solo visions become shared missions.

What We Want

The goal of the summit is to find the overlap in people's dreams and projects regarding bringing people together, using digital or in-person technologies. When mission aligned, 3 people working together have a greater output than 3 people working alone.

At the Cohesion Summit, we're gathering to:

  • Connect Toronto's community builders and organizers
  • Share what's working and learn from each other
  • Create a network of "superconnectors"; people who excel at bringing others together
  • Design tools and practices that make community building easier
  • Build things together that we can all contribute to equally

Through shared envisioning and tapping into our collective consciousness, we will be able to coalesce goals and cooperate on them for a more connected and loving world.

Why Bring People Together?

We're facing a predicament; not just isolated problems but intersecting crises that require collective action. When we create robust interdisciplinary social networks, we foster a sense of safety and community.

This safety enables cooperation on goals for the greater good. When people feel held by their basin of community, they're more willing to send out beacons to others, creating ripple effects of connection and collaboration.

Our Approach

Whether through apps, platforms, or human systems, we're open to all solutions that create beacons drawing people into basins of community.

Aggregating the aggregators: We're superconnecting the superconnectors; gathering those who naturally bring others together to share knowledge and coordinate efforts. By creating unfakeable signals of trust, we can spark ripple effects throughout Toronto's communities.

Human as the technology: People serving as the recommendation engine, building a culture of aggregation where communities actively share what's happening. But we're also exploring digital tools that could amplify these human connections.

Together, we'll design systems that make care visible and create non-event based connections; building relationships in daily life, not just at formal gatherings.

Join Us

If you're someone who brings people together; whether through events, communities, or just being a good neighbor; we want you there. Let's coordinate our efforts and amplify our impact.

Together, we can build the connections that build community.
